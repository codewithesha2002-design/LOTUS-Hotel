Frequently Asked Questions

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations made within 48 hours will incur charges based on the booking terms.

Is WiFi available?

Yes, complimentary high-speed WiFi is available throughout the hotel in all guest rooms and public areas.

Do you have parking facilities?

We offer complimentary valet parking for all guests. Self-parking is also available at additional rates.

What time is checkout?

Standard checkout time is 11:00 AM. Late checkout can be arranged based on availability. Contact the front desk for details.
