Room Description

Our elegantly appointed Deluxe Rooms offer the perfect blend of comfort and luxury. Each room features stunning city views and access to world-class amenities.

Bedroom

The spacious bedroom features a premium king or twin bed with Egyptian cotton linens (1000 thread count), ensuring a restful night's sleep. Large windows provide natural light and panoramic city views.
